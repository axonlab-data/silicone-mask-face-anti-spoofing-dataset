# Silicone Mask Face Anti-Spoofing Dataset

12,500+ videos of **silicone mask attacks** with 18 high-detail masks and 40+ accessory combinations (wigs, glasses, beards) for **face anti-spoofing**, **liveness detection**, and **iBeta Level 2 certification** training. Recorded across 10+ iOS and Android devices in varied environments and lighting conditions

## Key Features

- **18 unique silicone masks** - high-detail 3D face replicas for maximum realism
- **40+ accessory combinations** - wigs, glasses, beards layered on masks
- **Active liveness features** - natural head movements and blinking
- **Multi-device** - iPhone 14/14 Pro/13 Pro, Galaxy S23, Pixel 7, Honor 70, and more
- **Varied environments** - offices, apartments, outdoor locations
- **3 lighting levels** - low, medium, bright for robust training
- **iBeta Level 2 compliant** - directly supports PAD certification requirements
